SDM 2.0 records the global incidence of violent and nonviolent self-determination movements, 1945-2020. The Map allows you to see what territories are claimed by self-determination movements, the periods in which they have been active, what claims they have made, and their involvement in armed conflict with the state. The About section contains further explanations, including how the data has been collected, by whom, and who has funded the project. You can download the entire dataset by clicking on Download.
